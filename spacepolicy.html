A look into U.S. Space Policy

1950's

October 4, 1957

Sputnik

This was the launch of the world's first artificial satellite into orbit.

July 29, 1958

National Aeronautics and Space Act of 1958

This act created the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA), and outlined their purpose. NASA was tasked with expanding human knowledge. They were also strictly limited to peaceful operations, so that space would not become the battlefield for World War III.
